Re-Focus

Do you have a clear objective?

Choosing one clear objective allows you to have a clear end-point.

Split the objective into small steps.

Small steps allow the task to be manageable. If the objective is split into small steps, progress can be split into visible "checkpoints".

Where are you now?

Losing focus happens when the current focal point is lost. Knowing where you are enables you to stay on track and mark your progress.
