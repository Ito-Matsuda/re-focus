Re-Focus.

Give yourself a clear objective.

Choosing a clear objective allows you to work efficiently.

The objective should be a small task that is an achievable part of a bigger goal.

How will you start?

Getting started is the hardest part.

Plan how you will start your task, and the task will be easier to complete.

Where are you now?

Losing focus happens when your current focal point is lost.

Knowing where you are enables you to stay on track and mark your progress.
